At Healthmentee, we are passionate about improving the accessibility and quality of mental healthcare. We believe that everyone deserves access to mental health support, regardless of their location or schedule. That's why we have created a user-friendly and secure web-based mental health app that allows you to connect with licensed therapists and mental health professionals from the comfort of your own home. Our team is made up of experienced mental health professionals who are dedicated to helping you achieve your mental wellness goals. We understand that taking the first step can be daunting, which is why we strive to make the process as simple and stress-free as possible. We are committed to providing a safe and confidential space for you to discuss your mental health concerns and work towards a healthier, happier you.

Therapy can be an effective treatment for a host of mental and emotional problems. Simply talking about your thoughts and feelings with a supportive person can often make you feel better. FAQ

Therapy can help you manage life's varied challenges and live a more fulfilled life. It can help you understand what you're feeling, why and how to cope. Just like visiting your doctor for regular wellness exams, or your dentist for checkups, meeting with a therapist can help keep your mental health in order.

After you sign up, we will match you to an available therapist who fits your objectives, preferences, and the type of issues you are dealing with. Different therapists have different approaches and areas of focus, so it's important to find the right person who can achieve the best results for you. We have found that we are able to provide a successful match most of the time; however, if you start the process and you feel your therapist isn't a good fit for you, you may elect to be matched to a different therapist.

Knowing that you can say anything to your therapist and it will remain in the room helps you feel safe and builds trust between you and the therapist. For this reason, all therapists are legally and ethically bound to keep their sessions confidential and not share with anyone else what was talked about.
